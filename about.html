About Us

Thaneesha Group is The Best Manufacturer Of Cow’s Pure Ghee in India,

Pure Ghee is popularly pronounced as desi ghee in India. We have our factory where pure ghee is manufactured from pure butter of cow’s milk cream and processed in our state of the art pure ghee production units under the observation and supervision of certified and experienced experts.

Thaneesha group is proudly manufacturing the Cows’ pure ghee in two popular labels, Parth and Gramdhenu. We are the fastest growing Pure ghee manufacturer in India and among the top manufacturer and suppliers of pure ghee in Western India. We have a big network of Authorised distributors and dealers and our Pure Ghee supplies also include hotels, banquets, restaurants, caterers, and sweet shops.

Furthermore, we have all the required food safety licences, We follow extreme hygiene measures at our production plant with various quality checks and safe packaging procedures, we use high quality milk cream due to that end product of our popular pure ghee gives the original taste and health values to our end consumers.

Our mission is to deliver safe and healthy Pure Ghee to each household and family and for better growth and nutritious values to growing kids as the pure ghee is one of the must-have and traditional essentials of every Indian's kitchen.

Both of our Labels Parth and Gramdhenu are loved and recommended by our consumers, and our family of consumers is only growing as the consumer's trust and love our Pure ghee.

For any sales, distributorship, or dealership related query or supply at your shop or restaurant, or hotel, you can simply fill the contact form and one of our experts will be in touch with you. We assure you of the high quality product as we are the best Cow’s Pure Ghee manufacturer in India.
